Nepal needs your help.

On April 25th, the worst earthquake in over 80 years struck Nepal, killing over 7000 people. The death toll continues to rise. Hundreds of thousands of people have been left homeless. Aftershocks continue to rock the country, further damaging already weakened infrastructure and buildings. People need your help. We will come and pickup relief items you can spare, and get them into the hands of those who need help.

Donation Information

Right now we can only accept donations of TENTS (帳篷), TARPAULINS (篷布), WATER PURIFIERS (淨水器). The airport in Kathmandu is already operating at maximum capacity. Anything sent that is not critical, will prevent critical items from entering the country. If you have none of these, please consider donating money.

Please ensure items are undamaged and durable.

Who will receive the donations?

We are working with Childreach Nepal who have always worked in the rural, mountainous regions of Nepal, and these have been hit especially hard. Two of the villages they work in, Shermathang and Manikharka in Sindupalchok, have been completely destroyed. They have yet to hear from their other villages.

Their staff and trustees in Kathmandu have been working around the clock to get clothing, food and medical supplies to those stranded in the rural villages.
